Tear Down 404: 
  Get to zero as quick as possible. Numbers are located north, south, east, and 
  west. Hit the corresponding arrow keys to subtract that number from 404. This 
  is a race against time. Failing to hit the correct numbers will take longer to 
  get to zero.

  Player can switch mathematic symbols using spacebar. Symbol are minus (-) and
  divide (/)

  Two game modes: 
    Marathon and Sprint. Sprint is how many times a player can tear down 404 in 
    60 seconds. Marathon is how fast a player can tear down 26 404s.

  Note: Player will need to see arrow's current value as well as upcoming value.
